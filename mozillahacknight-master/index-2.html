Moz Hack Day


Moz Hack Day is an initiative by Codestreet in association with Techspace USICT and Mozilla GGSIPU.

It is an opportunity for students to contribute to Mozlla Projects. It is a 12 hours hackathon which focuses on involving more student developers in the Open Source World.

Projects range in platform and application, including elements of web development, mobile applications, and many others.

Students work for around 12 hours on a programming project under the guidance of various Mentors on 9 December 2017.

Q. What is Moz Hack Day?

Moz Hack Day is an activity that the student performs as an independent developer.

Q. Where does Moz Hack Day take place?

USICT, GGSIPU , Delhi

Q. What should I prepare prior to the programm?

Technology with which you wanna to work. :)

Q. What do I get by completing Moz Hack Day?

Awesome goodies and swags :)

Q. Who can participate?

Any college or university students are welcome to participate! If you are passionate about programming and are interested in open source contribution

Q. What programming language(s) should I be knowing?

The programming language you need to know depends on project you are interested in working with.
